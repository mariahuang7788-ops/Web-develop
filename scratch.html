My Cosplay & Makeup Journey

I started my cosplay and makeup journey when I was 17 years old. What began as a passion quickly turned into a small business where I provided makeup services for clients. Over the past two years, I have worked with many people and earned thousands of dollars doing something I truly enjoy.

My Cosplay Work

Each cosplay requires creativity, planning, and attention to detail. Below is my general process when creating a cosplay look:

  1. Plan the character and design
  2. Gather materials
    • Makeup products
    • Wigs and styling tools
    • Costume pieces
  3. Apply makeup and assemble outfit
  4. Final photos and adjustments

My Makeup Business

Alongside cosplay, I have run a small makeup business for two years. I have taken clients for events, photoshoots, and special occasions.
